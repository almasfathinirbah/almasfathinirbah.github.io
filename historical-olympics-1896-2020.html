Historical of Olympics (1896 - 2020)

I learn to design dashboards about Olympics games using Microsoft Power BI. The dataset source is taken from Kaggle.

As we know that Olympics is the most prestigious Sporting Event. This year, the Tokyo Olympics has been held, lets have a look at the history of the Olympics games to get more insight of it.

These dashboards comprise of all the event played from 1896 Olympics Games to 2020 Olympics Games featuring all Male and Female Athletes from all over the world which has featured in the Olympics Games.

So, what you think which country would be leading the Medal in Olympics History?
